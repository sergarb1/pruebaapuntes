The 4 Golden Rules

No Subject Change

Sin "-s" en la 3ª persona. Nunca.

He musts ❌
He must ✅

The "TO" Forbidden

Sin "to" después del modal (excepto ought to).

I can to swim ❌
I can swim ✅

Direct Inversion

Sin "Do/Does". Solo cambia el orden.

Do you should? ❌
Should you? ✅

No Continuous

No admiten "-ing" ni tiempos compuestos.

I am musting ❌
I must ✅

Logic & Deduction

MUST (99%) Sure it is YES
MAY/MIGHT/COULD Possible (50%)
CAN'T (0%) Sure it is NO
"He has 10 cars. He must be rich."
"It's 40°C. It can't be snowing."

Ability & Skills

CAN (Present)

General skills. "I can swim."

COULD (Past)

General past skills. "I could run fast."

Specific Success

Use WAS ABLE TO for one-time successes in the past.

Advice & Suggestions

SHOULD

Standard advice.

"You should rest."

OUGHT TO

Moral duty / Formal.

"We ought to help."

HAD BETTER

Warning (Warning!).

"You'd better go now!"

SHALL I / WE

Offers & Proposals.

"Shall we dance?"

Obligation

MUST: Sientes que debes hacerlo (interno).

HAVE TO: Las normas te obligan (externo).

Prohibition

Mustn't: Está prohibido. ¡No lo hagas!

"You mustn't tell anyone."

No Necessity

Don't have to: No es necesario. Opcional.

"You don't have to pay now."

Politeness & Social Usage

Informal

"Can I borrow your pen?"

Polite

"Could I use your phone?"

Formal

"May I take a seat?"

Master Level: "Would you mind closing the door?" (Usa siempre -ING después de 'Would you mind')

Past Modals: The Time Machine

Structure: MODAL + HAVE + PAST PARTICIPLE (V3)

Should have

Regret / Arrepentimiento.

"I should have studied."

Must have

Past Logic / Lógica.

"He must have left."

Could have

Unused chance / Posibilidad.

"I could have won."

Needn't have

Wasted effort / Innecesario.

"You needn't have cooked."

The Spanish Learner's Error Wall

No digas "I can to swim". El modal y el verbo van pegados sin nada en medio.

No confundas Mustn't con "no tienes que". Recordatorio: es prohibición total.

Would no se usa para estados pasados (be/live). Usa Used to.

Pronunciación: No pronuncies la 'L' en Should, Could o Would. (/ʃʊd/, /kʊd/, /wʊd/).
