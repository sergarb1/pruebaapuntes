The Mechanics (Anatomy)

The Golden Formula

Subject + HAVE/HAS + V3

Remember: He, She, It always uses HAS.

The "Spy" Contraction

Don't confuse She's (She is) with She's (She has).

IS: She's happy.
(Next word is Adjective)
HAS: She's eaten.
(Next word is Verb V3)

The 3 Universes of Usage

1. The "Life Achievement" (Experience)

Think of it as a video game achievement. It doesn't matter when you unlocked it, only that you have it.

"I have been to Japan." (It's in my life album)

2. The "Open Window" (Unfinished Time)

If the time period is not dead yet (Today, This week, This life), use Present Perfect.

"I have worked hard today." (Today is not over)

3. The "Ghost Effect" (Result Now)

Something happened in the past, but the ghost (the consequence) is haunting you NOW.

"I have lost my keys." = "I can't enter my house NOW."
